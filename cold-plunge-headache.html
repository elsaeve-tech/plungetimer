Cold Plunge Headache

Some people experience headaches during or after cold plunging. This is often related to rapid temperature change and blood vessel constriction.

Why It Happens

Cold exposure can cause blood vessels in the head to constrict quickly. This may trigger discomfort similar to a “brain freeze.”

How to Reduce It

  • Enter the water gradually
  • Avoid submerging your head immediately
  • Control your breathing

If headaches persist, reducing intensity or duration may help.

Cold exposure can affect people differently depending on intensity and duration. You can explore how cold plunging impacts heart rate or review general safety considerations like whether cold plunging is safe.
