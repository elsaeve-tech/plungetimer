Cold Plunge Guide

A simple guide to cold plunging, including temperature, duration, frequency, and recovery considerations.

Cold plunging, also known as cold water immersion, involves immersing the body in cold water for short periods of time. It has become popular among athletes, sauna enthusiasts, and wellness communities interested in recovery and resilience practices.

If you're new to cold plunging, the most common questions usually involve how cold the water should be, how long to stay in, and how often cold exposure should be practiced.
