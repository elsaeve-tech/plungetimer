Cold Plunge Before or After Sauna

Most people use cold plunges after sauna sessions, but both approaches exist depending on goals.

Cold Plunge After Sauna

This is the most common approach. The body is heated during sauna use, and cold exposure provides a strong contrast, which many people find invigorating.

Cold Plunge Before Sauna

Some people use cold exposure before sauna to create a contrast cycle. This may feel more intense and is less common.

Which Is Better?

For most people, cold plunging after sauna is more comfortable and easier to tolerate. The sequence often depends on personal preference and routine.
